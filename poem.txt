The poem

< HERE STARTS THE POEM >

Roses are red, 
violets are blue.
Sugar is sweet
but not as sweet as you.

< STOP >

Roses are red,
violets are blue.
I hate poems
even more than I hate you.

< STOP >

Roses are red,
violets are blue.
I'm locked in the cellar
with pictures of you.

< THE END >
